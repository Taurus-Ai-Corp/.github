# TAURUS AI Corporate Contributor License Agreement

**Version 1.0**

This Corporate Contributor License Agreement ("Agreement") is for organizations contributing to TAURUS AI Corp ("TAURUS AI").

## For Corporate Contributors

If you are making contributions on behalf of a company or organization, please complete this Corporate CLA.

### Requirements

1. **Authorized Signatory**: Must be signed by someone with authority to bind the organization
2. **Employee List**: Provide list of authorized contributors
3. **Email Confirmation**: Send signed agreement to legal@taurusai.io

### How to Sign

1. Download the [Corporate CLA PDF](https://taurusai.io/legal/ccla.pdf)
2. Fill out all required fields
3. Have an authorized representative sign
4. Email to: **legal@taurusai.io**
5. We will confirm receipt and add your organization to our records

### Required Information

- Company Legal Name
- Company Address
- Authorized Signatory Name & Title
- List of Authorized Contributors (GitHub usernames)
- Company Domain (for email verification)

## Agreement Summary

By signing the Corporate CLA, your organization agrees to:

1. **Grant Copyright License**: Allow TAURUS AI to use contributions under our Triple-Plus licensing model
2. **Grant Patent License**: License any patents covering the contributions
3. **Represent Authority**: Confirm the signatory has authority to bind the organization
4. **Manage Contributors**: Maintain list of authorized contributors
